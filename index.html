Our Story

MADE WITH LOVE
FROM NATURE

Shivam Organic was born from a deep love of nature and a belief that the best things in life come straight from the earth. We are a small, family-run business committed to bringing you the purest organic products — grown, harvested, and handcrafted by our own hands.

From our honey bee farm where bees freely collect nectar from natural crops, to our home kitchen where we carefully blend soaps, shampoos, and oils using traditional recipes — every product carries the warmth of handmade care.

We believe that good health should be accessible to everyone. That's why our prices are kept honest, our ingredients are transparent, and our products are made fresh — never mass-produced, never compromised.

HOW WE MAKE IT

Every product you receive is the result of careful, natural processes — from our farm and home to your hands. Here's exactly how we do it.

🍯 📸 Add Photo / Video
🐝 Organic Honey

FROM FARM TO BOTTLE

  • 1Our honey bee boxes are set up on our own farm where bees freely collect nectar from crops like fennel, suva, sesame, and more.
  • 2Once the honeycombs are ready, the honey is extracted by our team using a dedicated extraction machine.
  • 3The raw honey is carefully filtered 2–3 times through fine sieves to remove any impurities while preserving all natural goodness.
  • 4The pure honey is then packed into clean bottles (250g or 500g) and sealed fresh for you.

🌸 Organic Soap

HANDCRAFTED AT HOME

  • 1We prepare a premium soap base combined with pure goat milk as the nourishing foundation.
  • 2Natural ingredients are added by variety — rose petals for Rose soap, kesuda flowers for Kesuda, real coffee for Coffee Tan Removal, and more.
  • 3The mixture is poured into unique molds to give each variety its distinctive shape.
  • 4Once set, every bar is carefully packaged in a box and prepared for delivery.

🌱 Organic Shampoo

HERBS TO HAIR CARE

  • 1Different herbs are hand-collected from nearby fields and carefully sorted and cleaned at home.
  • 2These herbs are combined with a natural shampoo base to create a rich, organic formula.
  • 3The blend is mixed thoroughly to ensure even distribution of herbal goodness throughout every bottle.
  • 4Finally packed in 400g bottles, ready to nourish your hair naturally.

🧅 Hair Oil

ROOTS TO RESULTS

  • 1Natural ingredients like onions are carefully selected and prepared at home.
  • 2The ingredients are blended into a carrier oil base through a slow-infusion process to extract maximum benefits.
  • 3Apply at night, massage gently into scalp, and wash off in the morning — stops hair fall and promotes healthy growth.
  • 4Packed in 100g bottles, pure and ready for daily hair care.
